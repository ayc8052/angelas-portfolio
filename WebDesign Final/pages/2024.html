This Spring 2024 semester, I took a Re-plasticing fabrications class. We learned about all the different types of plastic and one of them is Polyethylene Terephthalate. It can easily be softened and morphed. A lot of plastic water bottles are PET so I decided to use it because of its accessibility. I used a tutorial on Pinterest on how to properly use a lighter on plastic because it could easily burn and melt. I collected all my plastic water bottles from friends and whenever I would buy drinks.

I weirdly found the most challenging part was adding the fairy lights around the chandelier. It kept getting stuck and knotted around everything, so it required a lot a patience. I also found it difficult to balance the weight distribution on the wire when attaching the flower and jellyfish. Here is a link to a slide show presentation I made for my final.
